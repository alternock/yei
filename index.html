Frequently Asked Questions

🎁 What is Yei?

Yei is a startup that aims to help content creators and people in the online world receive earnings without losses. Our platform allows you to receive income through multiple channels without needing so many different platforms, centralizing your earnings in one place.

⭐ Is registration free?

Yes, registration on Yei is 100% free. There are no hidden costs or payment requirements to start using our platform and all its functionalities.

🚀 Do I need to unlock any function to receive earnings?

No, you don't need to unlock anything to start receiving earnings in USDT, PayPal, and Stripe. The payment system is automatically activated when you register, and you can begin receiving money immediately. Payments are made weekly once you've accumulated $10 USD.

⚡ How does the augmented reality technology work?

Our augmented reality technology allows you to create interactive digital experiences that your followers can enjoy through their mobile devices. You can create geolocated YeiBoxes in specific locations for your fans to discover, similar to Pokémon GO. Fans can exchange YeiBoxes among themselves in the same way, but they cannot leave YeiBoxes for creators in this format, thus protecting the privacy of creators.

YeiBoxes can include various types of exclusive content such as voice notes, videos, and attachments to private streaming between the fan and the creator, thus creating personalized and unique experiences.

💰 On which platforms can I receive payments?

Yei allows you to receive payments from Telegram, Zoom, and the Yei application itself. This means you can monetize your chats, video calls, streaming, and exclusive content from multiple channels, all centralized on a single platform.
